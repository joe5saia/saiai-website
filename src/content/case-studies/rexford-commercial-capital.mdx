## Context

Rexford Commercial Capital needed a site that felt credible to prospective clients,
supported lead generation, and gave the business a stronger digital foundation than a
basic brochure presence. The website needed to do more than explain the company. It
needed to help move qualified visitors into a real inquiry path.

## What SAIAI delivered

The engagement focused on building a fast marketing site, shaping the lead capture
flow, and instrumenting the key parts of the journey so the business could understand
what was actually happening once the site launched. The implementation emphasized
performance, clarity, and a cleaner growth systems baseline.

## Outcome

The result was a production website with a more credible front door, a usable lead
capture flow, and the core analytics needed to support future optimization. That gave
the client a stronger platform for both immediate outreach and longer-term marketing
iteration.
