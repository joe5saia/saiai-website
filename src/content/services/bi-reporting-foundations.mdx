## What this work is really for

This is not about building dashboards for their own sake. It is about making sure the
business can answer simple questions quickly and confidently: what is happening, what
is changing, and where the team should pay attention next.

## Typical collaboration

Most projects start with a short pass on the current reporting mess, followed by a
definition of the core metrics and direct implementation of the first usable views.
The result should be a reporting setup the team can trust, not a heavier layer of
noise.
