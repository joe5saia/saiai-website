## When this works best

This is a good fit when the business needs a better website now, not an extended
branding exercise that delays launch. The work stays focused on clarity, lead flow,
and a site the team can actually maintain after it goes live.

## Typical collaboration

I usually start by narrowing the core pages, the message, and the actions the site
needs to support. From there I move through design, build, launch, and a clean
handoff so the site is useful immediately.
