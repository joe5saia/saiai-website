## What this sprint is for

This sprint is for teams that want to test AI inside a real operating workflow, not
for teams that want a disconnected demo. The best starting point is usually one
workflow with clear friction, clear inputs, and a real business owner.

## Typical collaboration

I usually start with a short scoping pass, then move into direct implementation with
regular check-ins around the workflow owner, the data involved, and the success
criteria. The goal is to ship something useful quickly while still surfacing the
important integration, risk, and support tradeoffs.
