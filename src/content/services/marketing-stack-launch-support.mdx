## Where this usually starts

This usually starts with a business that has enough marketing activity to feel the
pain of a messy stack but not enough time to keep cleaning it up by hand. The first
goal is a cleaner lead path. The second is making sure the team can actually run it
after launch.

## Typical collaboration

I usually work with the person closest to lead intake, follow-up, and reporting. The
end result should be a practical system with cleaner handoffs, not a more complex set
of tools to babysit.
