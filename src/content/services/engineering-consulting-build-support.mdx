## When this works best

This is the catch-all service for technical projects that are important, scoped, and
stuck. It works well when the team needs one person to help define the approach,
carry the highest-leverage implementation, and get the project over the line.

## Typical collaboration

I usually start by narrowing the scope and the delivery path, then move into direct
build support, technical decision-making, and launch work. The point is to reduce
ambiguity and keep the project moving, not to wrap it in more process.
