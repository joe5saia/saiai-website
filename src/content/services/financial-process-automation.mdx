## What this engagement is for

This engagement is for finance teams that are losing hours each week to operational
tasks that could be automated with simple, well-built integrations. The best candidates
are processes that are repetitive, well-defined, and currently done by hand across one
or more systems.

## Typical collaboration

I start by mapping the current process with the people who actually do the work, then
identify the simplest automation that removes the most friction. Implementation is
direct and iterative — I build against real data, test with the team, and hand off
something that works without ongoing engineering support.
