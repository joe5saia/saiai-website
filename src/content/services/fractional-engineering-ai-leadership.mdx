## When this works best

This engagement is a fit when the team is moving fast enough that gaps in technical
leadership start showing up in delivery quality, decision-making, or AI planning.
It is especially useful during periods of growth, product change, or operational
complexity where the business needs stronger technical direction without the weight
of a full executive hire.

## Typical collaboration

The work usually combines weekly working sessions, written recommendations, async
support, and direct participation in planning or delivery reviews. SAIAI stays close
enough to the work to improve decision quality while keeping the engagement scoped
around the highest-leverage problems.
